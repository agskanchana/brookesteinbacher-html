ABOUT BROOKE

I am a contemporary visual artist whose work transcends traditional boundaries between abstract expressionism and figurative representation. My practice explores the delicate intersection of human emotion, natural forms, and urban landscapes through bold color palettes and dynamic compositions.

Each piece is a dialogue between conscious intention and subconscious expression, seeking to create visual narratives that challenge perception and evoke profound emotional responses.
